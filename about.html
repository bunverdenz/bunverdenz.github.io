about myself

And now you are here! Nice! hehe :p Ok, let's hear some of my story

It started from one day in my highschool during biology class. My professor asked us students.. "Why were we born? Are we going to spend day by day in the future doing nothing?" ... ??? What did you just said?, I was laughing in my thought and ignored it a bit, since I knew that my professor is also a researcher and he tried to persuade us to do some researches. BUT! 2 or 3 days later, I suddenly got shocked from a bad news about my relatives, that's when my thought about my own future came up. And that's when the question from my Biology Professor came up to my mind. So I imagined, what if one day I could not be able to do anything anymore, what if I have to live and spend my life day by day uselessly. Sounds dramatic huh? Nevertheless, the more I thought, the more I felt inspired. It even forces me to find more and more thing in my life to do. So I came up with the idea "I want to be a useful person for this world". I should contribute something to the world at least 1% before I won't be able to. And that's been my lifetime dream up until now. It might sound exaggerating, but you know I am serious about this because of what I experienced. And I am still following my dream.

One day I found out, the easy (maybe?) and especially fun for me to do is "Computer Science" It's fun for me to learn more and more online by myself, to pursue my goal, and to get interesting job. Yay! In particular, to contribute something to the world! These are what pushed me so far!
